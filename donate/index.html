Give Baltimore the gift of laughter! The Baltimore Improv Festival is produced by the Baltimore Improv Group, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization fulfilling its artistic mission by offering performances, classes, jams, education initiatives and community outreach. The Baltimore Improv Festival is just one such outreach effort.

It's not only your Festival attendance that will help cement Baltimore as a home for serious improv theater--it's also your financial donation. Your generous gift will will help defray the overhead costs of the Festival, such as venue rental, visiting performer fees, and publicity. Donors' names will be printed in the Festival program and on Festival web site.
