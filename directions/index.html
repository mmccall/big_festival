All Baltimore Improv Festival events take place at the Creative Alliance at The Patterson, 3134 Eastern Ave., Baltimore MD 21224. Parking is on the street.

The Patterson movie theater was built in 1910 and reopened as The Creative Alliance at The Patterson in 2003. The 180-seat festival theater is part of a multi-purpose arts center that also includes two galleries for contemporary art, a classroom, media lab, offices and live/work studios for 8 artists making The Patterson their home.

From Downtown
Follow Pratt Street East from Inner Harbor
Turn right on President Street
Turn left on Eastern Avenue
Follow Eastern Avenue thru Little Italy, across Broadway, and past Patterson Park on your left.
3134 Eastern Avenue is one block past Patterson Park on your left. (Intersection of Eastern Avenue and East Avenue)

From I-83
Follow I-83 South until it ends and becomes President Street
Turn left on Eastern Avenue
Follow Eastern Avenue thru Little Italy, across Broadway, and past Patterson Park on your left.
3134 Eastern Avenue is one block past Patterson Park on your left. (Intersection of Eastern Avenue and East Avenue)

From I-95
Take Exit 57 - O'Donnell Street, Boston Street, Travel Plaza - to O'Donnell Street heading west. (note: from I-95 heading north, bear right off of exit ramp, then turn left on O'Donnell; from I-95 heading south, simply bear right off exit onto ODonnell)
Go over a railroad overpass
Turn right on Conkling Street
Turn left on Eastern Avenue (traffic light)
Continue on Eastern Avenue for 5 blocks
3134 Eastern Avenue is on your right. (Intersection of Eastern Avenue and East Avenue)
